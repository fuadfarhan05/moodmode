About MoodMap

MoodMap is a layout for users to understand their own mental state of mind. Although it can be difficult also, sometimes it is much easier to identify physical pain and discomfort more than mental pain or discomfort due to the lack of visual understanding of it. That is Where MoodMap comes in! In simple terms MoodMap is for the user to navigate how they feel. In here you will find a easy to follow map. Where you can learn more about how you feel.
